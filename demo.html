This is the google-map-storyboard Polymer element. Inside a storyboard, there are scenes (google-map-scene), and inside each scene there is content. Notice that down the bottom it tells you how many pieces of content there are at this scene. Click on the marker to show the next piece of content.
This is the last piece of content for this scene. If you click on the marker again, you go back to the first piece of content. To proceed to the next scene, click the forward arrow or the play button at the bottom of the map.
Content can also be images. Please click on the marker.
To get started with polymer please visit: www.polymer-project.org
If an invalid address is given, the scene won't be shown. If the address is changed, the location of the scene will change in real time.
Now open up the developer tools if you are using Chrome.
You can set the current scene by changing the current attribute in the storyboard and setting it to the scene id you want to jump to. For example, jump two scenes back by setting current to "scenesWithImages".
Using the dev tools, you can also add and remove scenes on the fly.
